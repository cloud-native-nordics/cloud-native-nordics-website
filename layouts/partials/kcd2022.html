Kubernetes Community Days Nordics 2022 - canceled

We are sorry to inform you that we need to cancel Kubernetes Community Day Cloud Native Stockholm event with short notice.

The reason for canceling this event is that we tried to pull everything off in a really short time frame but now we are realizing that we need to plan this on a longer horizon to make it as great as it should be.

It was a hard decision to make, but we feel this is the right thing to do with respect for participants, sponsors and the people involved in organizing it.

Sorry for any inconvenience that this may have caused you.

Stay tuned for future events! Best regards.
