Scaling adoption of Cloud Native in the nordics


Cloud Native Nordics is a meetup alliance across the five countries in northern europe. We help each other with many things, some of them being; tooling, website, speakers, guidelines, workshops, branding, and much more. We are a community built and run by passionate people.

The mantra is; By the community for the community.
